
**Quick Overview:**
- Open an entry and click the Upload button
- Add before photos (required)
- Add after photos (required)
- Describe the action taken (required)
- Add multiple rows if documenting different areas
- Click Upload Photos to save everything

---

## How to Upload Photos

### Step 1: Open Upload Dialog
Click the **Upload** button on any entry to open the photo upload dialog.

> ðŸ’¡ **Quick Instructions Button**  
> Inside the dialog, you can click the blue `?` button (top-right) to see a short Quick Instructions popup.

### Step 2: Add Before Photos
1. Click **+ Add 1 or more photos** in the **Before Photos** section  
2. Select one or more photos from your device  
3. Photos will appear as thumbnails

### Step 3: Add After Photos
1. Click **+ Add 1 or more photos** in the **After Photos** section  
2. Select one or more photos from your device  
3. Photos will appear as thumbnails

### Step 4: Add Action Taken (Required)
Type a description of what action was taken in the **Action Taken** text area.

### Step 5: Upload Photos
Click the **Upload Photos** button to save all photos and action taken.

### Step 6: Save Data
After uploading photos, always click the **Save Data** button at the bottom of the table to save all your inputted data from the table.

## After Upload

### View Photos
- Click the **View** button (green) to see all uploaded photos organized by row

### Edit Photos
- Click the **Edit** button (blue) to add more photos  
- Existing photos are preserved

### Delete All Photos
- Click the **Reset** button (red) to remove all photos and remarks
